Company Profile

Our company specializes in an array of geomatics services to meet our cliental needs.

GIS Services

Our GIS specialist will provide services that meet ESRI's Spatial data standards and GIS Interoperability documentation. Over 20 years, network/path analysis and digital terrain modeling were at the forefront of our operations. These specialities play a vital role in geodetic network planning for finding efficient path finding in rough terrain. 

Geodetic Services

Our Geodetic services consist of researching, designing, constructing and the exploitation of various sites falling under the service of construction geodesy. Geodetic surveying also plays a significant role in our operations. This service consist of determining the exact location of specific points by means of a network of permanently established surface markers with precise coordinates

Cartography Services

Our cartography services constituate the designing of various maps for multiple purposes. These maps are produced utilizing industry standard software of ArcGIS and Autocad combined with industrial plotters to produce quality maps and plans for land surveyors, architects and various types of engineering.
